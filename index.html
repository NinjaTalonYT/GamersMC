What Is GamersMC?

GamersMC is a fun survival Minecraft Server. On there you can claim land, builds cities for others to join, and much more! It also has nice staff and an awesome community! Hope to see you there.

Claiming

What does claiming do? Claiming land will protect all blocks, chests, signs, animals, doors, appliances, etc. in your claim. This means that anyone who is not trusted on your land will not be able to place/break blocks, open chests, use your machines, use your farm or harm your animals.
You can claim land by first doing "/kit claim" then selecting to points of land with your golden shovel. After that do "/claim" and your land will then be protected. For additional commands go Here.
